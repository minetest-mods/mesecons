This effector makes a sound if powered and can be used for making music. Normally it makes piano sounds. The sound frequency can be changed by punching the block (only works for piano). There are some special sounds that depend on the block below:
Block BelowEffect
Glass or Obsidian GlassHi-hat
Any stoneKick
Chest or Locked ChestSnare
Any treeCrash
Any wooden planksLite Crash
Coal BlockExplosion sound
Lava SourceFire sound
Steel BlockPiano (high pitch, one octave higher than normal)
Any other blockPiano (low pitch)
